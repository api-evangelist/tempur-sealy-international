# Tempur Sealy International

Tempur Sealy International is one of the world's largest bedding providers, developing, manufacturing, marketing, and distributing bedding products under the Tempur-Pedic, Sealy, and Stearns & Foster brands. As a Fortune 500 company, Tempur Sealy operates a global network of company-operated and licensed stores, e-commerce channels, and retail partnerships.
